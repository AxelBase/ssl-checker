Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 13, 2025

1. We Collect Nothing

The AxelBase SSL Certificate Checker is a fully client-side tool. When you check a domain, no data leaves your browser to us. We do not collect, store, log, or process any information about you or your queries.

2. What Happens When You Use the Tool

You enter a domain → JavaScript runs in your browser → a public proxy forwards the request to the SSL API → results appear instantly. Your IP never reaches the SSL API — only the proxy sees it.

3. Zero Tracking Technologies

We do not use:

  • Cookies or localStorage
  • Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any trackers
  • Session recording or heatmaps
  • Server logs (because there is no server)

4. Third-Party Services (Anonymous Only)

We rely on two public services for functionality:

  • api.codetabs.com — CORS proxy (sees proxy IP only)
  • ssl-checker.io — certificate data provider

Neither service receives identifiable information about you. We have no control over their logging policies.

5. Your Data Rights

Since we collect nothing, there is:

  • No data to delete
  • No account to close
  • No privacy request needed
Just close the tab — and you're gone.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date.

7. Contact

Questions? Reach out via GitHub Issues on the repository.

Privacy isn’t a feature — it’s the foundation. That’s why AxelBase SSL Checker will always remain 100% private by design.
