Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 13, 2025

At AxelBase SSL Checker, your privacy is not just respected — it is guaranteed by design. This tool was built from the ground up as a 100% client-side web application, meaning nothing you enter ever leaves your browser.

We Collect Absolutely Nothing

We do not collect, store, log, or transmit:

  • Domain names you check
  • Your IP address
  • Browser or device information
  • Timestamps or usage patterns
  • Cookies, localStorage, or any tracking pixels

All SSL certificate lookups are performed directly in your browser using a public CORS proxy and API. Even the proxy sees only anonymized requests — we have no access to them.

How Your Data Stays Private

When you use AxelBase SSL Checker:

  • All processing happens locally using JavaScript
  • No backend servers are involved in data handling
  • No analytics (Google Analytics, Plausible, etc.)
  • No session persistence — refresh = clean slate

This architecture ensures zero data retention and maximum privacy — even in the unlikely event of a breach, there is nothing to steal.

Third-Party Services

The only external request is to a public CORS proxy and the ssl-checker.io API — both are used solely to fetch certificate data. These services may log requests for abuse prevention, but we do not control or receive this data.

Your Rights & Compliance

Because no personal data is collected, we are compliant by default with:

  • GDPR (EU)
  • CCPA/CPRA (California)
  • LGPD (Brazil)
  • PIPEDA (Canada)

No consent banners. No cookie notices. No data subject requests needed.

Children’s Privacy

This tool is safe and appropriate for all ages. No personal information is ever requested or stored.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if new features are added. Any changes will be clearly announced on the site.

Your trust is everything.
That’s why we built a tool that proves privacy isn’t just a promise — it’s code.
